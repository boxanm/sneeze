Effie Daum is currently a Ph.D candidate in Norlab laboratory supervised by Prof. François Pomerleau at Université Laval. She has a master's degree in Geomatic from Ecole Supérieure des Géomètres-Topographes (Le Mans, France). She did her 6 months intership of her master's thesis in Norlab. Her research interests include 3D mapping, SLAM, and mobile robotics.
